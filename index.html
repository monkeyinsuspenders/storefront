Yes, even the worst ids are scannable, it is very simple to make a scannable id. Some bars use advanced scanners to detect fake ids that cost hundreds to thousands of dollars, and not every state passes on those. You can press on this link to see which states pass on which fake id detection systems. (Note that Intellicheck is typically only used by banks and government). All of the recommended IDs in the dropdown below pass common fake id detection systems.

There is no one best state, it depends on the area where you live. A mississippi ID will draw a lot of attention if used in Maine, and a Vermont ID would look very out of place in Missouri. What are considered the most accurate states currently are:
  • Arizona
  • Colorado
  • Indiana
  • Michigan
  • Mississippi
  • Tennessee
  • United Kingdom
  • Vermont

Most of the non recommended IDs are roughly the same quality, but New Jersey, Massachussets, and Kentucky should be specifically avoided. New Jersey is close to here and very commonly faked, so bouncers will easily tell the differences. Kentucky and Massachussets both have a security feature called a kinegram, which is nearly impossible to replicate, so they will be easily spotted as fake by a competent bouncer.
